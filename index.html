About Us

Founded in 2025 in Nairobi, Wambui EK Law Advocates is a progressive and modern law firm committed to delivering top-tier legal services across a broad spectrum of practice areas. Our core focus lies in public policy, human rights—particularly social, economic, and cultural rights— including food and health; environmental and climate justice; property and land rights; and women and child rights. Led by our founding advocate, Dr. Elizabeth Wambui Kimani-Murage, a distinguished expert with extensive experience in justice promotion, public health policy, food systems, environment, and climate change, our team comprises highly motivated associates with specialized expertise. Together, we are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of legal practice, enhancing societal impact, and transformative change in the justice experience for society.
